Hi, I' m Anna Kazanecki

Teacher and Creator from The Midwest

Welcome to my portfolio! This project is part of SheCodes, an organization that provides coding workshops for women. When I'm not learning, I have a former street dog that insists on going hiking with me and I love making things. I've been an elementary school teacher for 11 years and for the first time ever, next year I will be working with primary students in second grade! I'm excited for a change but I know that my time in the classroom will not be as long as I originally intended. I'm hoping to use my creativity and problem solving that I learned as an educator in whatever my next adventure may be. Until I have a specific plan, I'll be making timesaving teacher tools, designing logos, and creating materials to alleviate some of the stress that teachers are all feeling right now.
